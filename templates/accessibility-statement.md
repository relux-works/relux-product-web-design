## Technical approach

Accessibility relies on semantic HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WAI-ARIA where native HTML is
insufficient, and the accessibility APIs provided by supported browsers and operating
systems.

We combine automated testing with keyboard, zoom, contrast, forced-colors, localization,
and screen-reader review of critical workflows.
